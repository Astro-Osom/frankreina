Biography

I am a filmmaker and storyteller residing in Los Angeles.

I spent my early years in San Juan, Puerto Rico, and my professional years in the trenches of Hollywood. I've been a talent agent and a production assistant on two Christopher Nolan movies (The Dark Knight Rises, Interstellar), worked under legendary producer Robert Evans (Chinatown), and served as the right-hand man to action director Spiro Razatos (maestro of the physics-defying car chases for the Fast & Furious franchise).

My name is on the credits of movies like Captain America: Civil War, Venom, and the Coen Bros all-star movie Hail, Caesar!

I make movies (like my feature, Scrambled), I write, and I lead initiation rituals for men. My current focus is Future Vibrant Self — a project that is part book, part film, and part digital experiment. It's the result of my own process of falling apart and putting myself back together… and I couldn't have done it without yoga.
