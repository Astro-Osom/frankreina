Biography

I am a filmmaker and storyteller living in Los Angeles. A man of Cuban blood, and Puerto Rican soil. My quest from the Caribbean to the trenches of Hollywood's biggest productions, overcoming numerous challenges along the way, has taught me one thing: storytelling transforms lives.

I've been a talent agent and a fly on the wall on two Christopher Nolan movies (The Dark Knight Rises, Interstellar), worked under legendary producer Robert Evans (Chinatown, The Kid Stays in the Picture), and served as the right-hand man to action director Spiro Razatos (maestro of the physics-defying car chases for the Fast & Furious franchise).

My name is on the credits of movies like Captain America: Civil War, Venom, and the Coen Bros all-star movie Hail, Caesar!

I make movies (like my feature, Scrambled), I write, and I lead initiation rituals for men. My current focus is Future Vibrant Self — a vibequest that is part book, part film, and part digital experiment — that tracks self-sabotage to personal transformation… and I couldn't have done it without yoga. Storytelling transforms lives.
